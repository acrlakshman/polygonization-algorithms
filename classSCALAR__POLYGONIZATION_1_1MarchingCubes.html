Returns normalized distance of the iso-surface intersection from vertex-1.

  • Usage:

    (v1) X <–(f)--> O <—(1 - f)—> X (v2)

Marching cubes algorithm on a single cube.

Normals at surface vertices are computed via interpolation of provided normals. Triangle normals are computed as an average of vertex normals.

Parameters
cube_verticesposition vectors of 8 vertices of a cube.
edge_idsids of 12 edges of a cube.
scalarsvector of size 8 with scalar values at all vertices of a cube.
normalsvector of size 8 with normal vectors at all vertices of a cube.
iso_alphavalue for which iso-surface needs to be extracted.
Returns
tuple of triangles and triangle corners(/vertices).
